LYFT 2019 Bike Ridership in San Francisco
by Richard Zheng
Dataset:
-Analysis based off datasets found on "https://www.lyft.com/bikes/bay-wheels/system-data"

Summary of Findings:
- On average trip durations were around 700 seconds.
- Subscribers made more trips than customers.
- Customers had longer trips than subscribers.
- Number of trips peaked in March and July but dipped in December.
- More trips are made on any given weekday when compared to weekends.

Key Insight:
- Trips made by customers took longer than trips made by subscribers.
